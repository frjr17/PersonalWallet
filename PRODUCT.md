# Product

## Register

product

## Users

One private owner managing day-to-day finances across cash, bank, savings, and credit accounts. The product should support quick entry on a phone as well as deeper review and maintenance on desktop.

## Product Purpose

Personal Ledger makes recording, organizing, and understanding personal finances calm and dependable. Success means the owner can capture an entry with minimal friction, trust every balance effect, plan with nested categories and budgets, and recover their data without relying on paid services or bank integrations.

## Brand Personality

Calm, personal, and precise. The interface should feel thoughtful and tactile without becoming ornamental, and serious about financial integrity without resembling enterprise accounting software.

## Anti-references

Do not copy BudgetBakers branding, proprietary text, or exact layouts. Avoid generic fintech gradients, dense spreadsheet-like screens, dropdown-heavy forms, decorative glass surfaces, and color-only status communication.

## Design Principles

- Make the next financial action obvious and fast.
- Prefer direct manipulation and visible choices over hidden menus.
- Show financial meaning, not merely stored values—especially for liabilities.
- Preserve context through clear hierarchy, progressive disclosure, and reversible actions.
- Treat trust, accessibility, and balance integrity as product features.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA with complete keyboard operation, visible focus, semantic controls, screen-reader labels and status announcements, non-color status cues, reduced-motion support, and readable contrast in both light and true-black dark themes.
